An interesting experience of booking a room on Airbnb.

<!--more-->

This summer, I found an internship in Beijing and needed to find a room to live in. One of my friends recommended me to use Airbnb, a website for people to rent out lodging.[^1]

So I began to look through Airbnb, however it was not very easy to find a proper room. In brief, distance and price could not satisfy me at the same time. Hmm, but to tell the truth, most things are expensive in Beijing. At last, I chose a room near Wudaokou metro station for ¥189/day. The host of the house is Russian and I shared the whole house with two roommates — one is an American and the other is a Chinese Canadian. They are all nice people and their Chinese is much better than my English :) It was quite free to talk with them.

As I needed to find an independent space to prepare for my exam, I lived there for about 10 days. It was quite an interesting experience to live with them.

This is the [link](https://zh.airbnb.com/rooms/6226315) of the room, strongly recommended :)
