Booking Room On Airbnbby luvex

An interesting experience of booking room on Airbnb

This summer, I found an internship in Beijing and then, I need to find a room to live in. One of my friends recommended me to use Airbnb, which is a website for people to rent out lodging.1

So I began to look through on Airbnb, however it is not very easy for me to find a proper room on it. In brief, distance and price can not make me satisfied at the same time. Hmm, but to tell the truth, most of the things are expensive in Beijing. At last, I choose a room near Wudaokou metro station which is ¥189/day. The host of the house is a Russian and I share the whole house with two roommates, one is an American and the other is a Chinese Canadian. They are all nice people and their Chinese are much better than my English:) It is quite free to talk with them.

As I need to find an independent space to prepare for my exam, I live in their room for about 10 days, it is quite an interesting experience to live with them.
